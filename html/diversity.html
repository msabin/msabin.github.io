A student must be able to see that they can succeed as themselves. If their faces are not reflected, if their values are not reflected, if their culture is not reflected, then they will begin to hide pieces of themselves. They will not feel attached to academia or their success in it.

The many diversity recruitment activities I've engaged in can be found in my CV but the cultural and systemic changes I believe are needed for retention can be found in my Diversity Philosophy.

I am a queer, genderqueer Mexican and I believe getting more marginalized voices into a field and having an environment for them to bring their full selves is the most impactful way to make a field evolve.
